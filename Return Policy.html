Our Return Policy

At For Your Taste, we take pride in delivering authentic Malaysian cuisine made with the finest ingredients. We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase.

Please review our return policy below to understand your options if you're not completely satisfied with your order.

Food Items

  • Due to the perishable nature of our products, we generally do not accept returns of food items unless they arrive damaged, spoiled, or incorrect.
  • If you receive a damaged, spoiled, or incorrect item, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery with photos of the product and packaging.
  • We may offer a replacement, credit, or refund at our discretion after reviewing your case.

Non-Food Items

  • Non-perishable items (cookware, utensils, etc.) may be returned within 14 days of delivery if unused and in original packaging.
  • You will be responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error.
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method once we receive and inspect the returned item.

Refund Process

  • Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days.
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.
  • Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Contact our customer service team at returns@foryourtaste.com within the specified time frame.
  2. Provide your order number and details about the product you would like to return.
  3. Include photos of the product and packaging if applicable.
  4. Wait for approval and return instructions from our team.
  5. Ship the item back to us if required (for non-food items only).

Need Help?

If you have any questions about our return policy, please don't hesitate to contact us.
