Shipping Policy

Learn about our shipping methods, delivery times, and policies to ensure your orders arrive fresh and on time.

Shipping Overview

At For Your Taste, we take pride in delivering our authentic Malaysian delicacies to your doorstep with care and efficiency. We partner with trusted courier services to ensure your orders arrive fresh and in perfect condition.

Domestic Shipping (Within Malaysia)

  • Standard Shipping: 2-4 business days (RM8 flat rate)
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days (RM15 flat rate)
  • Free Shipping: Available for orders over RM100

Note: Delivery times may vary slightly during peak seasons or public holidays.

International Shipping

We currently ship to selected countries. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination:

  • Southeast Asia: 3-7 business days (RM30-50)
  • East Asia: 5-10 business days (RM40-60)
  • Middle East: 7-14 business days (RM50-80)
  • Europe & North America: 10-21 business days (RM80-120)

Please contact us for specific rates to your country and for any customs/duty information.

Order Processing

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and public holidays). You will receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order has shipped.

Freshness Guarantee

We take special care in packaging our products to maintain freshness during transit. All perishable items are packed with ice packs and insulated packaging when necessary. However, we recommend refrigerating items immediately upon receipt.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on the courier's website.

Delivery Issues

In the rare event that you experience issues with your delivery:

  • Check the tracking information for updates
  • Contact the courier directly with your tracking number
  • If unresolved, contact our customer service team within 3 days of delivery

We will work with the courier to resolve any delivery issues and ensure you receive your order or a proper refund.
