About For Your Taste

Bringing authentic Malaysian flavors to your table since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate food enthusiasts, For Your Taste began as a small family-run business in Kedah, Malaysia. What started as a humble stall at local markets has grown into a beloved brand known for its authentic Malaysian flavors.

Our journey has been fueled by our love for traditional recipes passed down through generations and our commitment to using only the finest ingredients.

Our Mission

To preserve and share the rich culinary heritage of Malaysia by delivering authentic, high-quality food products that bring people together and create memorable dining experiences.

Our Vision

To become Malaysia's most trusted brand for authentic local cuisine, recognized globally for our commitment to quality, tradition, and innovation in Malaysian gastronomy.

Our Core Values

Authenticity

We stay true to traditional recipes and cooking methods that have defined Malaysian cuisine for generations.

Quality

We source only the finest ingredients and maintain rigorous quality standards in every product.

Community

We believe food brings people together and are committed to supporting local farmers and producers.

Innovation

While honoring tradition, we continuously improve our products and processes to meet modern needs.

Sustainability

We're committed to environmentally responsible practices throughout our supply chain.

Customer Satisfaction

Your enjoyment is our top priority. We stand behind every product we sell.
